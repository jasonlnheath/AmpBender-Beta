AmpBender

Based on the legendary Marshall JCM800 circuit. Our goal: discover new tones hiding within this classic design. Bend your tone to perfection.

Signature Feature

The Bending Patchbay

Bend your signal like never before. Access and route gain stages directly to push the amp to its absolute limit. The interactive patchbay lets you visualize your signal path and load custom impulse responses for cabinet simulation.

  • HIGH/LOW input selection with visual cable routing
  • Drag-and-drop IR loader with waveform preview
  • Built-in 4x12" cabinet impulse response
  • Real-time signal flow visualization

Features

🎸

Tube Amp Simulation

State of the art tube amp modelling using advanced Wave Digital Filters (WDF), based on research from Stanford University and open source code from ChowDSP.

⚙

Circuit Mods

We incorporated some of the most popular mods for the JCM800 so you can push even further. Gain Boost and Input Warmth switches included.

⚡

Power Supply / VARIAC

Simulate voltage sag and VARIAC control for authentic tube compression and brown sound characteristics.

🎙

HIGH/LOW Input

Switch between high and low gain inputs just like a real amp. Interactive patchbay visualization included.

🔊

IR Cabinet Loader

Load your own impulse responses or use the built-in 4x12" cabinet. Drag and drop WAV files for instant tone matching.

📈

31-Band Spectrum

Real-time frequency analyzer shows your tone at a glance. Visualize the impact of every adjustment.
